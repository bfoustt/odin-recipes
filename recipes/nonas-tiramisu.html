Nona's Tiramisu Recipe

Description

Very simple, and very tasty recipe for Nona's Tiramisu dessert.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • basil
  • tomatoes
  • ricotta
  • onion
  • mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Chop Basil
  3. Sautee tomatoes and onion.
  4. Cool Pasta
  5. Stuff with ricotta and mozarella and chopped Basil
  6. Cover in tomatoe and onion sauce.
  7. Top with Mozarella
  8. Salt and Pepper
  9. Bake in Oven at 375 degrees for 20 minutes
